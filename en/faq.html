Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our mystery shopping services, customer service quality audits and research processes. If you can't find the answer to your question, contact us directly.

What is mystery shopping?

+

Mystery shopping is a research method in which trained auditors act as regular customers and evaluate service quality, sales standards and overall customer experience at a given company or establishment.

How long does the audit process take?

+

The duration of the audit depends on its scope. A standard direct audit takes 1-2 hours, while you receive a complete report within 3-5 business days from the completion of the study.

Are auditors trained?

+

Yes, all our auditors undergo specialist training in mystery shopping, service quality assessment techniques and the industry in which they will conduct research.

What industries do we serve?

+

We conduct audits in various industries: retail, food service, banking, insurance, telecommunications, automotive, medical services, hotels, beauty salons and many others.

Do you offer audits throughout Poland?

+

Yes, our services are available throughout the country. We have auditors in most provincial cities and smaller towns throughout Poland.

What research methods do you use?

+

We offer direct audits (visits to establishments), indirect methods (telephone and email research), website audits and customer satisfaction surveys.

What does the audit report look like?

+

The report contains a detailed assessment of all aspects of customer service, specific improvement recommendations, point ratings and comparison to industry standards. The report is prepared in electronic format.

Do you guarantee anonymity?

+

Absolutely. Auditor anonymity is crucial for research credibility. Our auditors are trained to maintain naturalness and do not reveal their identity during the study.
